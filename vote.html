The Charges

The defendants are charged with multiple violations of the Electoral Confidence Preservation Act (ECPA), a statute enacted for the stated purpose of preserving public confidence in electoral processes. The indictment alleges that each defendant, through public broadcast and written publication, did knowingly disseminate statements tending to undermine confidence in the integrity of elections.

Your Duty

It is your duty to weigh the evidence presented against each defendant and to render a verdict of Guilty or Not Guilty.

You are instructed that the defendants are presumed innocent. This presumption remains with the defendants throughout the trial and is not overcome unless the evidence convinces you of guilt beyond a reasonable doubt.

The Law

You are the judges of the facts. Your duty is to apply the law as written and as explained by an officer of this Tribunal, and to determine whether the facts established by the evidence satisfy each element of the charged offenses.

Weigh the evidence presented. Consider only what has been admitted before this Tribunal. Render your verdict according to whether the evidence, measured against the law as written, establishes guilt beyond a reasonable doubt.

I decline to serve as a juror

Your verdict has already been recorded. Thank you for your service.

Verdict Recorded

Your verdict has been sealed and recorded with the Clerk of Court. It will be read during the final proceedings.

Thank you for your service to the Tribunal.

EXCUSED
